Humidity

People say:"April showers bring May flowers". Based on City Latitude vs. Humidity chart 04/29/2018, it proves the poem is true. The month of April, traditionally a rainy period in Northern Hemisphere, gives way to May, when flowers will bloom because of the water provided to them by the April rains. In the Southern Hemisphere, April falls in Autumn, which is rainfall season as well. Overall, the humidity around the globe is relatively high in April.
